    High-Risk Transient Ischemic Attack or Minor Stroke

    In these patients (TIA with ABCD >= 4, minor stroke with NIHSS less than or equal to 3), dual anti-platelet therapy is warranted as per several recent trials (CHANCE1, POINT2 for clopidogrel and THALES3 for ticagrelor).

    Clopidogrel-based DAPT

    1. Load with ASA 162 mg PO and clopidogrel 300 mg PO
    2. ASA 81 mg PO and clopidogrel 75 mg PO daily for 21 days
    3. Antiplatelet monotherapy afterwards indefinitely
